Registration

Participants can be registered by email. Each group cannot have more than five group members (i.e., 1 to 5 group members is OK) and more than two instructors (i.e., 1 to 2 instructors is OK), and each participant can only join one group. Each group can only submit one algorithm for final ranking.
Participants will fill out the registration form and email it to LimitIRSTD@outlook.com.
Email title: ”ICPR2024 LimitIRSTD_(Track name)_(Team name)”
For Track 1: Weakly Supervised Infrared Small Target Detection Under Single Point Supervision, the track name is abbreviated as “Track1-Weak”.
For Track 2: Weakly Supervised Infrared Small Target Detection Under Single Point Supervision, the track name is abbreviated as “Track2- Lightweight”
